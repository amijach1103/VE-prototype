Here's where most people get stuck: they write actions that sound good but can't actually be measured.

"Get customers" isn't an action — it's a wish. "Talk to 5 potential customers this week" is an action. See the difference?

For each goal, I want you to write actions with numbers. If you can't put a number on it, you can't track it.
