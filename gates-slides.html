Script

Before I show you the system, let me be clear about what AI can and can't do.

AI can't replace peer connection ‚Äî but it can surface good matches. It can identify peers with complementary skills or similar challenges who might make good collaborators.

AI can't enforce accountability ‚Äî but it can set structure. Deadlines, nudges, helping people see how completing certain actions connects to their own goals.

AI can't build someone's confidence ‚Äî only people decide what they're capable of. But it can draw from research on motivation and adaptive resilience, delivering known interventions when engagement seems to falter.

And AI can't replace cultural context ‚Äî the deep knowledge of a specific community. But it can acknowledge its limits and point users toward community resources and local expertise.

The point: AI can't replace these important elements, so we're building it to facilitate them as much as possible.

This is the Vibe system ‚Äî built directly on what we learned works.

We analyzed over 800 documents from The Foundry. Four principles stood out: peer accountability, document discipline, weekly rhythm, and cohort community.

Each principle maps to a system component. Peer accountability becomes the peer connection layer ‚Äî the outer ring that AI facilitates but can't replace. Document discipline is built into the AI Coach, which structures written outputs. Weekly rhythm becomes automated nudges. And cohort community drives our peer matching features.

The AI Coach is our current focus ‚Äî it structures thinking, challenges vague ideas, clarifies goals. Admin support comes later. And capital: Vibe doesn't provide it, but we help entrepreneurs become capital-ready with clear plans funders want to see.
