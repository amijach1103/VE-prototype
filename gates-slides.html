Why entrepreneurship?

The expertise that matters is hard to scale. It's not in a textbook. It's knowing when to push back on a vague idea, what question to ask, when to say "you know your community better than I do." We think AI can learn these coaching patterns.

This is the Vibe system ‚Äî designed to preserve what works while making it accessible.

Peer connection is the outer layer. It was the highest-rated element from The Foundry, and AI can't replace it. Trust comes from shared experience.

Inside that: nudges that maintain the weekly rhythm. The AI Coach ‚Äî our current focus ‚Äî structures thinking, challenges vague ideas, clarifies goals. Admin support comes later, reducing friction on legal and marketing tasks.

And capital. We acknowledge it explicitly. Vibe doesn't provide capital, but we help entrepreneurs become capital-ready ‚Äî with clear plans that funders want to see. The capital comes from CDFIs, community lending circles, and local programs.

Script

This is the AI Coach we're building ‚Äî what we call the 5-7-5 Framework.

What AI does: pushes back on vague ideas, suggests specific actions like "talk to 5 potential customers this week," draws cross-domain examples, admits limitations upfront, uses plain language.

What AI can't do: replace peer connection, enforce accountability, make introductions, provide cultural context, build confidence. Those require humans.

AI should know its limits and say so.

12

Script

Our design principle: AI should know its limits and say so. When it acknowledges what it can't do, it builds trust ‚Äî and points users toward human support instead of pretending to have all the answers.
