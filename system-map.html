Build Your Plan
Week 1 — 30-45 min with AI Coach
5 moments: Idea, Mission, Goals, Actions, Plan. The AI Coach challenges vague thinking, pushes for specificity, and is honest about what it can't do. At the end, you have an actionable business plan — and a nudge toward the humans you actually need.

Phase 3 — Weekly Rhythm
Saturday Report
Weeks 2-12 — Reflect on your week
Every Saturday, you write a short management report: what you said you'd do, what you actually did, where you're stuck, and what's next. The Participant Bot auto-creates it from last week's commitments and tracks your changes over time.
