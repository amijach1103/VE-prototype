Before We Start

Do you have a business idea you'd like to work on? It can be something you're already doing, thinking about starting, or just exploring.

It doesn't need to be perfect - even a rough idea works!

Step 1: Your Mission

Let's start by capturing what your business is about. A good mission statement explains:

  • Who you help
  • What you do for them
  • Why it matters

Don't worry about making it perfect - just get your thoughts down.

AI
AI Assistant
Maria's Mission
"I help busy working parents in my neighborhood eat healthier by preparing affordable, homemade meals they can pick up weekly - because families deserve good food without the stress of cooking every night."

Try to keep it to 1-2 sentences.

Step 2: Your Goals

Now let's think about what you want to achieve. Good goals are:

  • Specific - Clear about what you'll do
  • Meaningful - Important to your success
  • Achievable - Something you can actually do

Most businesses focus on 3 main goals to start.

AI
Based on Your Mission
Maria's 3 Goals
1. Build my meal prep skills and recipes
2. Find my first paying customers
3. Set up reliable operations (kitchen, supplies, schedule)
